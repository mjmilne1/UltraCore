The objectives of this Investment Program are to:

  • Income Objective: Generate sustainable income through dividends and distributions
  • Tax Objective: Maximize after-tax returns through franking credits and CGT optimization

The Investment Program will implement a Strategic Asset Allocation approach with the following characteristics:

  • Diversification across multiple asset classes and geographies

  • Currency hedging on defensive assets only
  • Use of low-cost ETFs and index funds
