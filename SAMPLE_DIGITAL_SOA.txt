RISKS TO CONSIDER
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

All investments carry risk. Key risks include:

MARKET RISK
Description: Portfolio value will fluctuate with markets
Impact: Potential for negative returns, especially short-term
Mitigation: Diversification across assets and regions

TECHNOLOGY RISK  
Description: Reliance on algorithms and systems
Impact: Potential system errors or outages
Mitigation: Robust testing, backups, and monitoring

MODEL RISK
Description: Algorithms based on historical data
Impact: Past performance may not predict future
Mitigation: Regular model updates and human oversight

LIQUIDITY RISK
Description: Some assets may be hard to sell quickly
Impact: Delays in accessing funds
Mitigation: Maintain 5% cash allocation

CURRENCY RISK
Description: International investments subject to FX
Impact: Returns affected by exchange rates  
Mitigation: Partial hedging of international bonds

INFLATION RISK
Description: Returns may not beat inflation
Impact: Reduced real purchasing power
Mitigation: Growth assets and real assets allocation

REGULATORY RISK
Description: Changes to tax laws or regulations
Impact: May affect returns or strategies
Mitigation: Regular reviews and adjustments
