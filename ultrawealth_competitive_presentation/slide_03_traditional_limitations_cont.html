Traditional Robo-Advisors: Two Additional Limitations

► 04. LIMITED PERSONALIZATION
Personalization limited to risk profile selection. No customization of individual holdings, factor tilts, or tax optimization strategies. Behavioral analysis and learning from investor actions is absent.
► 05. ESG SCREENING VS. OPTIMIZATION
ESG investing through negative/positive screening (filtering out "bad" assets). Treats sustainability as a constraint rather than an optimization objective. Cannot generate "ESG alpha" through integrated optimization.
Critical Gap: Traditional robo-advisors are sophisticated calculators, not learning systems. They execute predefined strategies efficiently but cannot discover better strategies through experience.
