UltraWealth's Paradigm Shift: From Rules to Learning

DIMENSION TRADITIONAL ROBO-ADVISORS ULTRAWEALTH
ALGORITHMS Static Modern Portfolio Theory with manual recalibration by human portfolio managers → Deep Reinforcement Learning agents that continuously adapt through trial and error
PERSONALIZATION 3-7 template portfolios based on risk questionnaire scores → Individual RL agents trained for each investor's unique profile and goals
OPTIMIZATION TIMING Periodic rebalancing on fixed schedules (daily, weekly, monthly) → Real-time continuous optimization with opportunistic rebalancing
ESG INTEGRATION Negative/positive screening filters applied as constraints → Dual-objective optimization maximizing both returns and ESG outcomes
AUDITABILITY Point-in-time database snapshots with limited historical reconstruction → Kafka event sourcing with complete immutable audit trail and time-travel
Fundamental Difference: Traditional robo-advisors execute predefined strategies efficiently. UltraWealth discovers optimal strategies through continuous learning and adaptation.
